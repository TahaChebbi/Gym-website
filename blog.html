OUR COVID-19 HEALTH COMMITMENTS

A cover, it is preparing for the 8 JUNE!
Although we are extremely attentive to the cleanliness of our club on a daily basis, our duty to you is to reinforce, even more, the measures put in place usually.
BE FIT IS COMMITTED:
We are always very attentive to the cleanliness of our club, we take advantage of this time to clean and disinfect every surface and every machine so that you can continue to live the best of #Fitness from the start!
In accordance with the decision of the Ministry of Youth and Sports, we have prepared a reopening plan as follows:
✅ Welcome!
• Inlet temperature measurement
• Footwear disinfection bins at entrance
• Badge your cards
• Locker room closures
• Mask required for employees and direct clearers
• Make disinfectant gel available to customers and employees
✅Cardio, weight training and classrooms!
• Wear your towels
• Require use of gloves
• Provide customers with sanitizer sprays and paper to disinfect machines after each use
• Limit the number of customers in the courses by respecting the safety distance
• Limit the number of members in the club at the same time and ensure physical distancing
• Full daily club disinfection
